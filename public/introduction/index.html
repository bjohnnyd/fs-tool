    Introduction

    This document is meant to be a simple guide to the technical aspects of using fs-tool. It is not meant to cover the underlying idea of fs-tool nor why you might want to use it; for this you should read our paper, Debebe et al Identifying the immune interactions underlying HLA class I disease associations eLife 2020; 9:e54558

    None of it will make much sense unless you have read the paper first! If you have any questions/problems/comments then please get in touch with Becca Asquith or Bisrat Debebe. Please do not forget to cite us if you use fs-tool or the underlying ideas:

    Debebe et al. eLife 2020; 9:e54558 doi: 10.7554/eLife.54558).
